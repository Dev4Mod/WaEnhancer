# WA Enhancer

A powerful Xposed/LSPosed module that supercharges your WhatsApp experience with advanced privacy, customization, and utility features. Unlock the full potential of your messaging app with tools designed for power users.

### üîí Privacy & Security
*   **Anti-Revoke:** Read messages even after they were deleted by the sender. Never miss a "This message was deleted" again.
*   **Anti-View Once:** View "View Once" photos and videos unlimited times. They don't disappear after you close them.
*   **Hide Read Receipts:** Read messages without sending blue ticks, while still seeing others' blue ticks.
*   **Hide Delivery Reports:** (Experimental) Prevent the second tick (delivery) from showing to the sender.
*   **Hide Seen Status:** View statuses/stories anonymously perfectly. The sender won't know you watched.
*   **Typing Privacy:** Hide your "typing..." status in chats and groups.
*   **Recording Privacy:** Hide your "recording audio..." status.
*   **Call Privacy:** Control who can call you (Everyone, My Contacts, Nobody, etc.).
*   **Locked Chats Enhancer:** Security improvements for locked chats (biometric limits).

*   **Hide Tabs:** Remove tabs you don't use (e.g., Communities, Calls) for a cleaner UI.
*   **Instagram-Style Status:** View statuses in a horizontal list like Instagram stories.
*   **Menu Home:** Customize the home screen menu options.
*   **Custom Font:** Apply custom fonts to the application.

## üì¶ Installation
1.  **Root** your Android device.
2.  Install **LSPosed** (Zygisk or Riru version).
3.  Download and install the **WA Enhancer** APK.
4.  Open the **LSPosed Manager** app.
5.  Enable the **WA Enhancer** module.
6.  Ensure **WhatsApp** is selected in the scope (it should be auto-selected).
7.  **Force Stop** WhatsApp.
8.  Open WhatsApp and verify the module is active (look for the enhancement menu).
